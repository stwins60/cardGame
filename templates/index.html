📝 How to Play

  • Enter a room name and click "Join Room" to enter the game lobby.
  • Once all players have joined, click "Start Game" to begin.
  • Each player receives a hand of cards.
  • Play your cards strategically when it's your turn.
  • Score points based on the game rules (e.g., card values or matches).
  • The player with the highest score at the end wins!
  • Use "Leave Room" to exit the game at any time.
